## üÜï 4. New Release Section - Detailed Specifications

### **Purpose**
- Create excitement for new product launches
- Build anticipation with countdown and pre-order
- Showcase manufacturing transparency and quality
- Keep homepage fresh and dynamic

### **Section Layout**

**Full-width section positioned after "Top Shelf Picks" and before "Ingredient Philosophy"**

### **Content Structure:**

**Headline:** "Introducing [Product Name]"
- Large serif typography (Playfair Display)
- Animated fade-in on scroll

**Subheadline:** "Our latest innovation in [category]"
- Smaller sans-serif (Inter)
- One-line compelling benefit

**Left Column (60%):**
- **Factory Manufacturing Video** (20-30 seconds)
  - Shows manufacturing process
  - Clean, modern facility aesthetic
  - Ingredient ‚Üí mixing ‚Üí filling ‚Üí packaging ‚Üí final product
  - Builds trust and transparency
  - Auto-play on mute, loop
  - Play/pause controls on hover

**Right Column (40%):**
- **Product Image** (large, hero shot)
- **Key Benefits** (3-4 bullet points)
- **Hero Ingredient Callout** (ingredient card style)
- **Launch Info:**
  - "Launching [Date]" or "Available Now"
  - Countdown timer (if pre-launch)
  - Limited edition badge (if applicable)
- **CTA Button:**
  - "Pre-Order Now" (if pre-launch)
  - "Shop Now" (if available)
  - "Notify Me" (if sold out)
  - Glowing button effect matching brand aesthetic

### **Additional Elements:**

**Bottom Section:**
- Small carousel of product detail shots (3-4 images)
- Ingredient spotlight
- Before/after results (if applicable)
- Early access messaging ("Be the first to try")

### **Design Specifications:**

**Background:**
- Soft gradient matching new product color
- Subtle texture overlay
- Premium, elevated feel

**Typography:**
- Headline: 48px Playfair Display
- Subheadline: 24px Inter Regular
- Body: 16px Inter Regular
- CTA: 18px Inter Medium

**Spacing:**
- Generous padding (80px top/bottom)
- Clean white space around elements
- Breathing room for premium feel

**Motion:**
- Video auto-plays on scroll into view
- Fade-in animations for text
- Hover effects on CTA
- Smooth transitions

## üéÆ 5. Grab the Deals Section - Detailed Specifications

### **Purpose**
- Create interactive, playful shopping experience
- Showcase daily specials or featured products
- Gamification of product discovery
- Viral-worthy, shareable content
- Nostalgic arcade vibe meets premium skincare

### **Section Layout**

**Full-width section positioned after "New Release" and before "Ingredient Philosophy"**

### **Content Structure:**

**Headline:** "Grab the Deals"
- Large, playful serif typography (Playfair Display)
- Subtext: "Daily Specials" in smaller font
- Could also be "Daily Steals" or "Deal of the Second"

**Subheadline:** "Choose your prize ‚Äî limited time only"
- Creates urgency and excitement
- Playful, inviting tone

**Center/Hero Area (70%):**
- **Claw Machine Video** (15-20 seconds, loop or interactive)
  - Arcade-style claw machine aesthetic
  - Top-down or angled view
  - Multiple Lumore products visible as "prizes"
  - Mechanical claw descends and grabs featured product
  - Clean, bright aesthetic (not dark arcade)
  - Products look premium even in playful context
  - Video can rotate through different products daily

**Side Panel (30%):**
- **Today's Featured Product** (the product being grabbed)
  - Product image
  - Product name
  - Special offer price / discount badge
  - Timer showing "Offer ends in: [HH:MM:SS]"
  - Quick add to cart button
  - "Grab it now" CTA

### **Interactive Elements:**

**Option A: Auto-Play Video**
- Video loops continuously
- Shows different products being grabbed in sequence
- Smooth transitions between grabs

**Option B: Click-to-Grab**
- User clicks "Try to Grab" button
- Claw animation plays
- Grabs random product from daily specials
- Shows product details after successful grab
- More interactive, gamified experience

**Option C: Hover Animation**
- Hovering over different products triggers claw movement
- Claw hovers over hovered product
- Click to "grab" and see product details

### **Design Specifications:**

**Background:**
- Soft gradient (pastel arcade aesthetic)
- Could use retro arcade patterns (subtle pixel art or geometric)
- Modern twist on arcade nostalgia
- Premium feel maintained

**Typography:**
- Headline: 54px Playfair Display (playful serif)
- Subheadline: 20px Inter Regular
- Product name: 28px Inter Medium
- Price/Timer: 24px Inter Bold
- CTA: 18px Inter Medium

**Color Scheme:**
- Soft pastels (pink, blue, yellow - arcade vibe)
- Premium accents (gold for "special offer")
- Clean whites for clarity
- Product colors remain accurate

**Spacing:**
- Generous padding (60px top/bottom)
- Centered video with breathing room
- Side panel aligned and balanced

**Motion:**
- Video auto-plays on scroll into view
- Countdown timer ticks in real-time
- CTA button pulse/glow effect
- Smooth claw movements (not jerky)

### **Product Rotation:**

**Daily Rotation:**
- Different product featured each day
- "Monday: Solar Veil", "Tuesday: Dew Drops", etc.
- Creates repeat visit behavior
- Fresh content daily

**Special Events:**
- Flash sales (limited quantity)
- New product launches
- Seasonal promotions
- Bundle deals

### **Responsive Behavior:**

**Desktop (1440px+):**
- Video center, side panel right (70/30 split)
- Full claw machine view
- All details visible

**Tablet (768px - 1439px):**
- Stack to single column
- Video on top (full width, reduced height)
- Product details below video

**Mobile (< 768px):**
- Single column stack
- Video reduced height (maintains aspect ratio)
- Simplified product details
- Full-width CTA button
- Timer more prominent

### **Integration with Asset Generation:**

This section requires:
- **Section 3, Video 3** (Claw Machine Video)
  - Multiple variations showing different products
  - Can create 7 versions (one per product)
  - Or one master video with all products
- **Product Renders** (already completed)
- **Daily Special Badge Graphics**
- **Countdown Timer Component**

### **Content Management:**

**Dynamic Fields:**
- Featured product (changes daily/hourly)
- Discount percentage
- Timer duration
- CTA text
- Video variant (which product is grabbed)

**Allows easy updates for:**
- Daily specials rotation
- Flash sales
- Limited-time offers
- Seasonal promotions

### **Engagement Features:**

**Analytics Tracking:**
- Click-through rate on "grab" CTA

- Popular products (which get grabbed most)

**Social Sharing:**
- "I grabbed [product]!" social share option
- Screenshot/share successful grab
- Referral incentives

### **Marketing Messaging:**

**Taglines:**
- "Grab the Deals" (Daily Specials)
- "Every day, a new prize"
- "Can you grab today's deal?"
- "Limited prizes, unlimited glow"
- "Test your luck, grab your glow"

**Tone:**
- Playful, fun, nostalgic
