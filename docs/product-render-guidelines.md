# Lumore Product Render Guidelines

**Purpose:** Standard guidelines for generating consistent, premium product renders across all Lumore products.

---

## **Render Philosophy**

Lumore product renders follow a clean, minimal, premium aesthetic:
- Pure white backgrounds for versatility
- Flat lighting to showcase product colors accurately
- Matte frosted finishes for tactile, premium appeal
- No distracting shadows, reflections, or effects
- Focus on the product itself

---

## **Standard Render Specifications**

### **Background**
- Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- No shadows
- No gradients
- No color contamination from lighting

### **Lighting**
- Flat, even studio lighting
- No directional shadows
- No reflections on matte surfaces
- No highlights or specular effects
- Consistent across all products

### **Material Finish**
- Opaque matte frosted plastic (primary)
- Velvety soft-touch appearance
- NO glossy or shiny surfaces
- Frosted transparent glass (serum bottle only)
- White matte for all caps/pumps/dispensers

### **Composition**
- Product centered in frame
- Front view primary (facing camera directly)
- Standing upright on base
- 1:1 square format for individual products
- 16:9 horizontal format for product lineups
- 8K photorealistic quality

### **Camera & Framing**
- Eye-level view (not looking down or up)
- Product fills 60-70% of frame
- Equal space around product
- Sharp focus throughout product
- No depth of field blur (unless creative variation)

---

## **Product-Specific Guidelines**

### **Bottles (Pumps & Sprays)**
- Pump/spray dispenser matches body diameter exactly
- Capless design (no separate caps)
- White matte dispensers
- Angled pump tops (lower one edge, higher opposite)
- Text label spread vertically across upper 2/3
- Bottom 20% reserved for pattern (if applicable)
- Pattern flush with bottom base

### **Dropper Bottles**
- Frosted transparent glass body
- Internal dropper visible through glass
- Liquid visible at 80% fill level
- Dropper bulb color matches body color
- No cap (exposed dropper)

### **Jars**
- Geometric shapes (octagonal, hexagonal, dome)
- White matte lids
- Lid sits flush on top
- No bottom patterns on small jars

### **Tubes**
- Soft squeezable plastic appearance
- Natural taper (wider at bottom, narrower at shoulder)
- NOT rigid cylinder
- White matte screw caps
- Very thin proportions for lip products

### **Dome Containers**
- Rounded spherical dome shape
- Wider than tall (squat proportions)
- White matte dome caps
- Sitting on surface (not floating)

---

## **Typography & Labeling**

### **Label Hierarchy**
1. "lumore" (brand) - small, upper area
2. [Product name] - larger, middle area
3. [Category/description] - smaller, below name
4. [Volume] - smallest, lower area

### **Text Specifications**
- Cool grey color (matches across all products)
- Lowercase serif font style
- Good vertical spacing between elements
- Text follows container curves naturally
- Spread across upper 2/3 of container
- Leave bottom 20% for patterns (if used)

---

## **Bottom Patterns (When Used)**

### **Pattern Guidelines**
- Occupy bottom 20% of container height
- Two horizontal rows of small icons
- Icons in WHITE color
- Pattern FLUSH WITH BOTTOM BASE (no gap)
- Icons evenly spaced within rows
- Simple, minimal icon designs

### **Pattern by Product**
- solar veil: Small sun icons (circle with rays)
- dew drops: NO PATTERN
- lumiskin: Layered horizontal wave lines
- reset mist: Circular ripple patterns
- melt away: NO PATTERN
- kiss proof: NO PATTERN
- under cover: NO PATTERN

---

## **File Naming Convention**

### **Individual Products**
`[number]-[product-name]-[product-type].png`

Examples:
- `01-solar-veil-sunscreen.png`
- `02-dew-drops-serum.png`

### **Lineup Images**
`00-full-product-lineup.png`
`00-lineup-variation-[number]-[description].png`

Examples:
- `00-full-product-lineup.png`
- `00-lineup-variation-01-pastel-gradient.png`

### **Starter Packs & Combos**
`starter-[name].png`
`combo-[name].png`

Examples:
- `starter-essentials.png`
- `combo-hydration-duo.png`

## **Quality Standards**

### **Resolution**
- Minimum 2048×2048px for individual products
- Minimum 3840×2160px for lineup images
- 8K quality when possible
- PNG format with transparency (if needed)

### **Consistency Checks**
- [ ] Colors match hex codes exactly
- [ ] White background is pure #FFFFFF
- [ ] No shadows or reflections (unless creative variation)
- [ ] Matte finish maintained throughout
- [ ] Text is legible and properly spaced
- [ ] Proportions match product specifications
- [ ] Product centered in frame

---

## **Creative Variations (Optional)**

When creating styled/lifestyle versions:
- Document base render settings first
- Add shadows, backgrounds, or props intentionally
- Maintain product color accuracy
- Keep product as hero (don't let background dominate)
- Use soft, premium lighting
- Follow brand aesthetic (minimal, elevated, clean)

Examples:
- Pastel gradient backgrounds
- Natural minimal styling (pampas grass, stones)
- Architectural modern styling (geometric shapes, arches)

---

## **Usage**

These renders will be used for:
- E-commerce product pages
- Homepage hero sections
- Product carousels & grids
- Social media posts
- Marketing materials
- Print assets
- Packaging mockups
- Lifestyle composites

**Key Principle:** Create clean, versatile base renders that can be used across all marketing channels while maintaining consistent brand identity.
