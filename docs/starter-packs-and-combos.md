## **Packaging Guidelines**

### Ritual Sets:
- Premium rigid box with magnetic closure
- Individual products nestled in custom insert
- Routine instruction card (AM or PM timing, order of application)
- Gift-ready presentation with branded tissue paper
- Box exterior matches ritual theme:
  - Morning Ritual: Bright, sunny colors (yellows, oranges, soft blues)
  - Evening Ritual: Calming colors (lavenders, peachy corals, soft greens)

### Duos:
- Branded sleeve wrapper holding products together
- Hang tag with "Better Together" messaging
- Product pairing benefits listed on back
- Lighter packaging than ritual sets
- Easy to add to existing orders
